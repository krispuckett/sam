# Weekly Rhythm

---

## Monday: Planning & Alignment

**Morning**
- Open weekly briefing
- Set ONE Thing for the week
- Review team's priorities

**Afternoon**
- Manager 1:1 (if scheduled)
- Team sync/standup

**End of Day**
- Week's shape is clear

---

## Tuesday-Thursday: Execution

**1:1 Distribution**
- Spread across Tue/Wed/Thu
- Don't stack more than 3 per day
- Leave buffer between for notes

**Critiques**
- Mid-week preferred
- When energy is stable

**Stakeholder Meetings**
- As needed
- Prep required

---

## Friday: Review & Reset

**Morning**
- Process week's notes
- Update team files
- Clear inbox

**Afternoon**
- Weekly review (see template)
- Prep next week
- Close loops

**End of Day**
- Week is closed
- Next week is visible

---

## Weekly Commitments

| Commitment | Frequency | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| Manager 1:1 | 1x | Monday preferred |
| Team 1:1s | 1x each | Distributed Tue-Thu |
| Critique/Review | 1-2x | As work requires |
| Stakeholder touch | 1x key | Relationship maintenance |
| Weekly review | 1x Friday | Non-negotiable |
