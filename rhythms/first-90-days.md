# First 90 Days Playbook

**Start Date**: January 20, 2026
**Day 30**: February 18, 2026
**Day 60**: March 20, 2026
**Day 90**: April 19, 2026

---

## Phase 1: LEARN (Days 1-30)

### Mantra
> "Understand first. Change nothing. Build trust."

### Primary Activities
- Meet every team member 1:1 (multiple times)
- Meet stakeholders (introduction round)
- Learn the domain (Essential Journeys)
- Observe critiques (don't lead yet)
- Document questions, don't answer them yet

### Daily Focus
- Ask questions
- Listen more than talk
- Take notes obsessively
- Resist urge to fix things

### By Day 30
- [ ] Know each report's strengths, edges, goals
- [ ] Know manager's priorities
- [ ] Can explain team's current work
- [ ] Understand Essential Journeys at high level
- [ ] Stakeholder map draft complete
- [ ] Observations documented (not acted on)

---

## Phase 2: ALIGN (Days 31-60)

### Mantra
> "Validate hypotheses. Find quick wins. Draft priorities."

### Primary Activities
- Test observations with manager
- Identify low-hanging fruit
- Start giving substantive feedback
- Draft team priorities hypothesis
- Build on stakeholder relationships

### Daily Focus
- Synthesize what you've learned
- Look for patterns
- Small wins build credibility
- Start having opinions (but hold them lightly)

### By Day 60
- [ ] Validated or revised initial observations
- [ ] One quick win shipped
- [ ] Feedback is flowing naturally
- [ ] Draft priorities shared with manager
- [ ] Team trusts you enough to be honest
- [ ] Know who's an ally, who to watch

---

## Phase 3: BUILD (Days 61-90)

### Mantra
> "Execute. Establish rhythm. Ship something."

### Primary Activities
- Lead critiques confidently
- Ship meaningful work (through team)
- Establish sustainable rhythm
- Address one systemic issue
- Deepen key stakeholder relationships

### Daily Focus
- Act on what you've learned
- Your opinions matter now
- Rhythm > heroics
- Delegation is leadership

### By Day 90
- [ ] Team has shipped something you shaped
- [ ] Sustainable weekly rhythm established
- [ ] One systemic improvement made
- [ ] Manager trusts your judgment
- [ ] You're not burned out
- [ ] Next quarter's priorities are clear

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### Days 1-30
- ❌ Changing processes
- ❌ Giving strong feedback too early
- ❌ Making promises you can't keep
- ❌ Working on IC tasks to prove yourself

### Days 31-60
- ❌ Ignoring red flags you noticed
- ❌ Avoiding hard conversations
- ❌ Over-committing to stakeholders
- ❌ Neglecting self-care

### Days 61-90
- ❌ Still not having opinions
- ❌ Doing work your team should do
- ❌ Unsustainable hours
- ❌ Ignoring manager relationship

---

## Weekly Check-in Questions

### Days 1-30
- What did I learn this week?
- Who haven't I talked to yet?
- What am I assuming that might be wrong?

### Days 31-60
- What hypothesis did I validate/invalidate?
- Where's the quick win opportunity?
- What's the one thing I'd change?

### Days 61-90
- What shipped this week?
- Is this pace sustainable?
- Am I delegating or doing?
