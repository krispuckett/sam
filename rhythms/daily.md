# Daily Rhythm

---

## Morning (First 30 Minutes)

1. **Check energy state** — Green/Yellow/Red
2. **Open briefing** — `sam`
3. **Set ONE Thing** — What makes everything else easier?
4. **Scan calendar** — What's coming? Any prep needed?
5. **Quick inbox triage** — Anything urgent? (Don't go deep)

---

## During the Day

### Before Each Meeting
- Check prep notes (if 1:1 or stakeholder meeting)
- Clear prior meeting's mental residue

### After Each Meeting
- Capture key points immediately (don't trust memory)
- Log feedback given/needed
- Update action items

### Between Meetings
- If >30 min gap: Do focused work
- If <30 min gap: Administrative tasks, Slack, email

---

## End of Day (Last 15 Minutes)

1. **Close briefing**
   - What shipped?
   - What carried?
   - What learned?

2. **Prep tomorrow**
   - Review tomorrow's calendar
   - Note any prep needed
   - Set preliminary ONE Thing

3. **Clear tabs/notes**
   - Process capture into right places
   - Close open loops or note them

---

## Energy-Based Adjustments

### Green Days
- Load up with important work
- Schedule hard conversations
- Invest in relationship building

### Yellow Days
- Protect ONE Thing fiercely
- Keep conversations shorter
- Okay to be quieter

### Red Days
- Required meetings only
- Inbox triage
- No feedback delivery
- Flag if multiple Red days in a row
