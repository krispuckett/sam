# Monthly Rhythm

---

## Week 1: Team Health

- Review each report's status
- Check in on career conversations owed
- Update team dashboard
- Note any interventions needed

---

## Week 2: Stakeholder Maintenance

- Review stakeholder map
- Schedule catch-ups with neglected relationships
- Check manager relationship health
- Cross-functional pulse check

---

## Week 3: Quality & Craft

- Review recent critiques
- Essential Journeys quality check
- Note systemic quality issues
- Update domain knowledge

---

## Week 4: Review & Plan

- Monthly review (30 min)
- Update development focus progress
- Identify next month's priorities
- Career conversation (1 report)

---

## Monthly Commitments

| Commitment | When | Notes |
|------------|------|-------|
| Team health review | Week 1 | All reports |
| Stakeholder review | Week 2 | Key relationships |
| Quality review | Week 3 | Craft check |
| Monthly review | Week 4 | 30 min protected |
| Career conversation | Week 4 | Rotate through team |

---

## Monthly Questions

1. **Team**: Is anyone struggling I haven't noticed?
2. **Stakeholders**: Who needs more investment?
3. **Quality**: Is the work getting better?
4. **Self**: Am I still learning? Still sustainable?
5. **Manager**: What does my manager need to know?
