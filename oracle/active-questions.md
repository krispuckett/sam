# Active Questions

*Daily self-assessment questions. Rate 1-10.*

## Default Questions (Goldsmith's Daily Questions)

1. Did I do my best to set clear goals today?
2. Did I do my best to make progress toward my goals?
3. Did I do my best to find meaning?
4. Did I do my best to be happy?
5. Did I do my best to build positive relationships?
6. Did I do my best to be fully engaged?

## Custom Questions (Stripe Focus)

7. Did I do my best to be visibly present (not just connector mode)?
8. Did I do my best to protect my energy (not overextend to prove myself)?

---

## How to Use

During `oracle daily` sessions, rate each question 1-10. Track in daily-questions.csv.

The questions measure *effort* ("Did I do my best to..."), not outcomes. This focuses on what you can control.
